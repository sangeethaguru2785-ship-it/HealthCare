FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have about contacting us.

What is your response time?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our phone line directly.

Can I schedule a visit in advance?

Yes, you can schedule appointments up to 30 days in advance through our online booking system or by calling our reception desk.

Do I need a referral?

Most specialists do not require a referral. However, some insurance plans may require one. Please check with your insurance provider before your visit.

What documents should I bring?

Please bring a valid photo ID, insurance card, any previous medical records, a list of current medications, and referral letters if applicable.

Is there someone available to assist with language?

Yes, we have multilingual staff and translation services available in Spanish, Mandarin, French, and Arabic to assist non-English speaking patients.

Are your facilities wheelchair accessible?

Absolutely. All our facilities are fully ADA compliant with wheelchair ramps, elevators, accessible restrooms, and designated parking spaces.
